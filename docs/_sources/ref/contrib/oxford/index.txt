University of Oxford
====================

The University of Oxford deployment is at `http://m.ox.ac.uk/ <http://m.ox.ac.uk/>`_ and goes by
the name *Mobile Oxford*.

Apart from local configuration and such things as API keys, the entirity of
the Oxford deployment is kept in the Molly source tree (under
:mod:`molly.contrib.oxford`) in order that it may be used for inspiration or
as a reference at other institutions.

Mobile Oxford currently provides a variety of Molly applications.
