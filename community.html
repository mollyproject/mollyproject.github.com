The Community

Having a strong commitment to true open source community development, virtually all code is designed in the open and reported on mailing lists. This allows us to build the project as a community of like-minded individuals and organisations and makes sure the software is flexible, secure and sustainable for the future.

Starting in Q2 2010, the Molly project community is made up of the University of Oxford and Oxford Brookes University as well as significant contributions from individuals. If you would like to get involved in the community development or have any questions about the Molly project please e-mail mobileoxford@oucs.ox.ac.uk.
